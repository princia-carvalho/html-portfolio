Movie Recommendations by Princia

Top 3 Best Movies of all-time.

Main Hoon Na

In the film, Major Ram Prasad Sharma is sent on a covert mission to pose as a college student and protect the general's daughter from a dangerous rogue soldier.This movie has the best rom-com scenes with great songs and good action.

Golmaal Series

Golmaal is now the ninth-highest-grossing film series in Bollywood. The film series has been described as a screwball comedy franchise.The movie is the best family watch and full of comedy.

To all the Boys I have Loved Before Series

It's an high school romance film. And I love the videography and plot of the movie. It stars Lara Condor and Noah Centineo. This movie series consists of 3 movie. It's a good family watch as well.
